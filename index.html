Why another cryptocurrency?

Ragazzo fills a gap in the existing cryptocurrency landscape. With emission logic nearly equivalent to the widespread Bitcoin, and security/privacy features matching the world-leading Monero, Ragazzo meets every possible user demand. Fully decentralized, Ragazzo is secure and accessible for everyone.

Secure, fast, and high-tech

Ragazzo is based on the ultra-secure CryptoNote protocol, which protects against the double spend problem, uses ring signatures for fully anonymous payments, and creates one-time keys for unlinkable transactions.

With Ragazzo, sending money is as quick and painless as sending an email. Even across international borders, no matter what amount.

Ragazzo uses state-of-the-art cryptography to ensure every transaction is fully anonymous and untraceable.

Completely Decentralized

Ragazzo's community-powered nature means you don't have to trust anyone with your money.

Centralized Banks

In centralized banking, your money exists mostly in digital form. The ledger which keeps track of how much money you have is stored somewhere in a bank computer, and your entire personal finance rests in their hands. If someone were to hack the bank and edit this ledger, there would be nothing you can do.

Decentralized Money

Because every Ragazzo user hosts the blockchain (equivalent to a bank's ledger), even if one or many individuals are compromised, your funds will still be accounted for by many other users in the public blockchain.
